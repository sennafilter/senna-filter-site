About me

You might know me from @sennafilter.

I make content about AI for people who don't have "tech" anywhere in their title. What it actually is, what it's good and bad at, and how to use it at work and in real life.

By day, I work in AI strategy, identifying where and when AI actually makes sense inside a business. This is where I take everything I learn in those rooms and make it useful for everyone else.

My degree is in biochemistry. I taught myself tech from scratch and somehow ended up doing AI strategy for a living, none of it the expected way. So I know exactly what it feels like to figure out something dense and intimidating from scratch.

That's what I do here: translate. Whatever's confusing or hyped or buried in jargon, I take something that sounds intimidating and hand it back human and actually useful.
